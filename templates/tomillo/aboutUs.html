Fundation Tomillo

The Foundation aims to:

  1. Promote, support or undertake activities dedicated to facilitate the social and occupational inclusion of those disadvantaged or at social risk, through training programs and/or the requalification, guidance services and other initiatives.
  2. Promote, support, or undertake pre-school, child, and youth education activities in socially disadvantaged environments, to improve access to educational resources and prevent academic failure.
  3. Promote, support, or undertake other activities that, benefitting society, are directly or indirectly related to the aforementioned objectives, including cultural activities, study and analysis of social and economic reality, volunteer promotion, development of social economy and sustainability
